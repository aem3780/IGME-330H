About the Spectacular Sensational Sandbox

The Spectacular Sensational Sandbox is fun drawing application that utilizes canvas and JavaScript. Users are able to draw pictures with their mouse, make spunky random backgrounds and create pictures made of shapes. The sandbox is completely customizable, you can choose your background color, paint color, fill, shape, filters and brush sizes. If you make mistake, no worries, simply clear the canvas! Feel free to take screen captures of your masterpieces and get creative!
